About

TA leader who builds systems

I own R&D Talent Strategy and Talent Acquisition at Atlan — the active metadata platform that serves as the missing context layer for enterprise data and AI teams. My primary focus is hiring engineers and product managers — from IC to Staff to leadership — alongside Design, IT, and Security.

Over 20+ years in talent acquisition and talent strategy, I've designed and executed enterprise-scale talent systems across APAC, North America, and Europe. I've served as VP at ZTek Consulting advising Fortune 500 clients across 18 countries, led hyper-growth scaling at GreyOrange (150 to 700+ employees), and built TA organizations from the ground up. I've led 100+ senior leadership hires at Director/VP/C-suite level across technology and business functions.

What makes me different: I also build the tools my team uses. I design and ship evaluation frameworks that run as AI agents, native macOS widgets for API usage tracking, and Cloudflare Workers that generate personalized offer experiences — all open source, all built with AI. I believe the future of talent acquisition is programmable, and I'm building it alongside the day job.
